Topics

git’s Safety Scissors

The knack [to flying] lies in learning to throw yourself at the ground and miss.

Ford Prefect, The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

The sharpest tools make the job easiest, but also present a danger in themselves. Learn about the tools git provides to preclude and fix mistakes, so you'll have the confidence to use it the way it was meant to be used.

git Internals

Take a deeper look at the internals of git. By understanding how things work under the covers, you'll be more prepared to discover how to do what you're trying to achieve when you move beyond the basics. Rote memorization of a few "incantations" isn't a terrible way to begin, but you'll soon find yourself wanting to do more.

git Flow

Explore collaboration and workflows using git and GitHub. Discover new ways of working that git enables.

git Hacks

Host a blog, spy on your neighbors, stalk your favorite developers.

Share Your Experience

This is an intimate community event, and we want to hear about your experience with git and other source control systems. Bring your laptop and war stories!
